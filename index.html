🇯🇵 Ultimate 14-Day Japan Exploration Itinerary

Embark on a two-week adventure across Japan that promises an exhilarating blend of cultural immersion, culinary delights, and breathtaking landscapes.

✈️ Day 1: Departure from Beirut to Osaka

⏰ Wake-Up: 6:00 AM

🛫 Departure: 9:00 AM from Beirut–Rafic Hariri International Airport (BEY)

🛬 Arrival: 5:00 AM (Day 2) at Kansai International Airport (KIX)

Note: Flight duration is approximately 16 hours with a layover. Adjust rest accordingly during the flight.

🎏 Day 2-5: Osaka - The Kitchen of Japan

⏰ Wake-Up: 7:00 AM

📍 Location: Osaka

  • Day 2:
    • Arrival and check-in
    • Explore Dotonbori area
    • Evening street food tour
  • Day 3:
    • Osaka Castle
    • Shinsekai district
    • Kuromon Market
  • Day 4:
    • Universal Studios Japan
    • Osaka Aquarium
  • Day 5:
    • Day trip to Nara
    • Evening in Umeda Sky Building

🏨 Accommodation: Hotel in Namba/Dotonbori area

🍱 Foods to Try: Takoyaki, okonomiyaki, kushikatsu, kitsune udon

🎎 Day 6: Kyoto - Culture Capital of Japan

  • ⛩️ 10:00 AM: Explore Fushimi Inari Shrine.
  • 🎋 12:30 PM: Stroll through Arashiyama Bamboo Grove.
  • 🏯 4:00 PM: Tour Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion).
  • 🌃 8:00 PM: Explore Gion District at night.

🏨 Accommodation: Hotel in Kyoto.

🍵 Foods to Try: Matcha parfaits, obanzai, and kyo-kaiseki.

🌸 Day 7: Kyoto Deep Dive – Temples, Gardens, and Tea

  • ⛩️ 8:00 AM: Visit Kiyomizu-dera Temple with panoramic views of Kyoto.
  • 🛍️ 9:30 AM: Stroll Higashiyama District for souvenirs and traditional crafts.
  • 🍵 11:00 AM: Participate in a formal tea ceremony in a machiya townhouse.
  • 🏯 1:00 PM: Visit Nijo Castle – walk the nightingale floors.
  • 🏞️ 3:00 PM: Relax in the Kyoto Imperial Palace Park.
  • 👘 5:00 PM: Wear a kimono and take photos around Maruyama Park.
  • 🍲 7:00 PM: Enjoy a yudofu dinner near Nanzen-ji.

🏨 Accommodation: Same as Day 6 (Kyoto)

🍵 Foods to Try: Yudofu, Nishiki Market street snacks, sake tasting, shojin ryori

🦌 Day 8: Nara + Osaka – Sacred Deer & Neon Streets

  • 🚆 8:00 AM: Take the Kintetsu Limited Express from Kyoto to Nara.
  • 🦌 9:00 AM: Visit Nara Park and feed the famous sika deer.
  • ⛩️ 10:30 AM: Explore Todai-ji Temple and the Great Buddha.
  • 🌸 12:00 PM: Quick visit to Isuien Garden.
  • 🚆 2:30 PM: Head to Osaka.
  • 🎮 4:00 PM: Explore Den Den Town.
  • 🌃 6:00 PM: Experience the chaos of Dotonbori.
  • 🎭 8:00 PM: Optional comedy show at Namba Grand Kagetsu.

🏨 Accommodation: Cross Hotel Osaka or Mitsui Garden Hotel Osaka Premier

🍱 Foods to Try: Kakinoha sushi, takoyaki, okonomiyaki, kushikatsu

🕊️ Day 9: Hiroshima & Miyajima – Peace and Floating Torii

  • 🚆 7:00 AM: Take the Shinkansen to Hiroshima.
  • 🕊️ 9:00 AM: Visit the Peace Memorial Park, Atomic Bomb Dome, and Museum.
  • 🏞️ 11:00 AM: Stroll along Shukkeien Garden.
  • 🚢 2:00 PM: Ferry to Miyajima Island.
  • ⛩️ 3:00 PM: Visit Itsukushima Shrine and floating torii gate.
  • 🌅 6:00 PM: Watch the sunset at the torii gate.

🏨 Accommodation: Sheraton Grand Hiroshima Hotel

🍱 Foods to Try: Hiroshima-style okonomiyaki, momiji manju, oysters

🍜 Day 10: Fukuoka – Food Capital of Kyushu

  • 🚆 8:00 AM: Shinkansen to Fukuoka (Hakata Station).
  • ⛩️ 9:30 AM: Visit Kushida Shrine.
  • 🏘️ 11:00 AM: Walk through Hakata Old Town.
  • 🛍️ 12:30 PM: Explore Canal City Hakata.
  • 🗼 2:00 PM: Visit Fukuoka Tower.
  • 🍜 4:00 PM: Join a ramen-making workshop.
  • 🌃 6:00 PM: Experience the riverside yatai food stalls.

🍱 Foods to Try: Hakata ramen, mentaiko, motsunabe

♨️ Day 11: Beppu – Onsen Wonderland

  • 🚆 8:00 AM: Take Limited Express to Beppu.
  • ♨️ 10:30 AM: Start the Beppu "Hells" Tour.
  • 🛁 2:30 PM: Soak in a traditional onsen.
  • 🚡 4:30 PM: Visit Mount Tsurumi via ropeway.
  • 🛏️ 6:30 PM: Relax at ryokan with private onsen.

🍱 Foods to Try: Jigoku mushi, local seafood, onsen-pudding

🌄 Day 12: Takachiho Gorge – Myth & Nature

  • 🚆 7:00 AM: Travel to Takachiho.
  • 🚣 11:00 AM: Row a boat down Takachiho Gorge.
  • ⛩️ 12:30 PM: Visit Takachiho Shrine.
  • 🌌 2:00 PM: Explore Amano Iwato Cave.
  • 🎭 6:30 PM: Watch the Takachiho Yokagura dance.

🍱 Foods to Try: Chicken nanban, local soba, Kumamoto beef

🗼 Day 13-14: Tokyo Highlights

  • Day 13:
    • Morning: Senso-ji Temple & Asakusa
    • Afternoon: Shibuya Crossing & Harajuku
    • Evening: Tokyo Tower
  • Day 14:
    • Morning: Tsukiji Outer Market
    • Afternoon: Final shopping in Akihabara
    • Evening: Return to Osaka for departure

🏨 Accommodation: Hotel in Shinjuku area

🍱 Foods to Try: Sushi breakfast, ramen, monjayaki

Take evening Shinkansen back to Osaka for morning flight

👋 Day 14: Sayonara Japan

  • 🍳 8:00 AM: Breakfast at a local kissaten.
  • ⛩️ 9:00 AM: Final visit to Asakusa.
  • 🛍️ 10:30 AM: Last-minute shopping at Tokyo Station.
  • 🛫 11:30 AM: Head to the airport.

🍱 Foods to Try: Ekiben, Hokkaido milk soft cream, airport sushi

Flight: Tokyo → Beirut (approximately 15-17 hours total)
