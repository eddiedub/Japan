🗾 Extended 16-Day Japan Adventure

A more relaxed exploration of Japan's highlights, allowing for deeper immersion and flexibility.

✈️ Day 1: Arrival in Tokyo

📍 Location: Tokyo

  • 🛬 Arrival: Narita/Haneda Airport
  • 🏨 Check-in: Hotel in Shinjuku area
  • 🌃 Evening: Explore Shinjuku area

Suggested: Arrive by afternoon for easy check-in and evening exploration

📍 Location: Tokyo

  • 🏮 8:00 AM: Visit Senso-ji Temple
  • 👗 10:30 AM: Explore Harajuku
  • 🚶 12:30 PM: Experience Shibuya
  • 🗼 3:00 PM: Visit Tokyo Tower
  • 🌃 7:00 PM: Enjoy Shinjuku nightlife

📍 Location: Nikko

  • 🚂 7:00 AM: Take early train to Nikko
  • 🏮 9:00 AM: Visit Toshogu Shrine
  • 🌊 11:30 AM: Explore Kegon Falls
  • 🏞️ 2:00 PM: Tour Lake Chuzenji
  • 🌅 5:00 PM: Return to Tokyo

🍱 Foods to Try: Yuba dishes, local mountain vegetables

🗻 Day 4: Hakone Getaway

📍 Location: Hakone

  • 🚂 8:00 AM: Take Romancecar from Tokyo
  • 🎨 10:00 AM: Visit Open Air Museum
  • 🚡 1:00 PM: Owakudani Ropeway experience
  • ⛴️ 3:00 PM: Lake Ashi Cruise
  • ♨️ 6:00 PM: Evening onsen

  • 👘 6:00 PM: Explore Gion district
